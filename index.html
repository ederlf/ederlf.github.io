Biography

I am a Research Assistant and PhD candidate at the Networks group of Queen Mary University of London’s School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science. My research interest includes Software Defined Networking, network simulation and inter-domain routing. I have a developer background, having worked in Open Source projects such as the implementation of OpenFlow 1.3, Route Flow and in the development of the winner OpenFlow driver on a competition organized by the Open Networking Foundation.

Parsing the RTA_MULTIPATH attribute from Rtnetlink

I am developing an application that relies on the detection of changes to the Linux kernel route tables. For the job, one can use Rtnetlink for reading and modification of the routing tables. For example, the RTM_NEWROUTE message can be used to add a new route or read from the netlink socket, indicating a new route was added to the route table.
